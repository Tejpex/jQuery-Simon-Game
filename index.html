Press A Key to Start

When you start the game, the computer will flash a color.
You should press that color.
Then the computer will give you a new color.
You should now press the first color, then the new color.
If you were right, the computer will yet again give you a new color.
You should press the first color, the second color and then the third color.
And so on...
The game ends if you press a color out of order.
